What Information We Collect

When you sign up for our Inner Circle or programs, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Fitness goals and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Contact you about your fitness program and membership
  • Send you relevant fitness tips and gym updates
  • Improve our services and understand our members better

We will never:

  • Share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Sell your data
  • Send you spam

Email Communications

When you provide your email address, you consent to receive:

  • Program updates and booking confirmations
  • Exclusive offers for Inner Circle members
  • Fitness tips and community news

You can unsubscribe anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting Jude directly.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Your data is stored securely using Mailchimp (our email service provider)
  • We only keep your data as long as you're interested in our services
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS) on our website

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data - ask what information we hold about you
  • Correction - ask us to correct any incorrect information
  • Deletion - ask us to delete your information
  • Portability - receive a copy of your data
  • Objection - object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact Jude at jude@japourgym.com.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track how our website is used (via Google Analytics)
  • Track conversions from Facebook ads (via Facebook Pixel)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email.
